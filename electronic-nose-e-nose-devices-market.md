<p><p>The Electronic Nose (E-nose) devices market is classified into various types: Metal Oxide Sensors (MOS), Conducting Polymers (CP), Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW), and others. MOS-based E-nose technology uses semi-conducting metal oxides to detect and identify different gas odors. CP-based E-nose devices employ conducting polymers to detect and analyze volatile organic compounds (VOCs). QCM sensors measure mass changes through resonance frequency shifts caused by gas interactions. SAW-based E-nose devices use piezoelectric materials to analyze gas samples. The "Others" category includes alternative E-nose technologies like optical sensors, gas chromatography, and differential mobility spectrometry.</p></p>
