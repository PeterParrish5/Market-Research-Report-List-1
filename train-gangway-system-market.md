<p><p>The market for train gangway systems can be categorized into two types: single piece rail gangways and two piece rail gangways. Single piece rail gangways consist of a single continuous structure that connects train cars, providing a seamless passage for passengers and staff. On the other hand, two piece rail gangways are composed of two separate sections that can be connected or disconnected as needed. Both types of gangways contribute to enhancing passenger safety, comfort, and ease of movement between train cars.</p></p>
