<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors are devices that use the propagation of surface acoustic waves to detect and measure physical phenomena such as temperature, pressure, acceleration, and chemical concentrations. These sensors consist of a piezoelectric substrate that converts electrical signals into mechanical vibrations, which are then detected by interdigital transducers. SAW sensors offer various advantages such as high sensitivity, fast response time, compact size, and low power consumption.</p><p>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for miniaturized sensors across various industries such as healthcare, automotive, aerospace, and industrial.
