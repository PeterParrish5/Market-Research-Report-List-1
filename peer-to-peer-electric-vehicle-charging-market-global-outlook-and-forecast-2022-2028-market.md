<p><p>The Peer-To-Peer Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Market refers to the sharing of electric vehicle charging infrastructure between vehicle owners. Blockchain enables secure, transparent, and automated transactions between EV owners and station hosts. It also ensures the accurate measurement and recording of energy usage, allowing for fair compensation between parties.</p><p>Another trend in the peer-to-peer EV charging market is the utilization of smart charging technologies. These technologies optimize charging schedules based on energy prices, grid demand, and the preferences of vehicle owners.
