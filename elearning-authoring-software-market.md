<p><p>The e-learning authoring software market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. It offers greater flexibility, scalability, and accessibility since users can access the software remotely from any device with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises e-learning authoring software is installed and operated locally on the user's computer or server.
