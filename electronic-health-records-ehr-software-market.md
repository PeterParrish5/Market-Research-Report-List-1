<p><p>The Electronic Health Records (EHR) Software market can be categorized into two types: Cloud and On-premises. Cloud-based EHR software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, allowing healthcare providers to access patient records from any location. On the other hand, On-premises EHR software is installed and operated on the local infrastructure of healthcare providers, requiring them to manage and maintain the software on their premises. Both types have their advantages and considerations, such as cost, security, and customization, which healthcare organizations must consider when choosing a suitable solution.</p></p>
