Piezometers monitor groundwater pressure in order to understand its impact on the stability of structures. Rain gauges measure the amount of rainfall an area receives. Thermometers gauge the temperature of soil and rock. Other devices encompass various instruments like inclinometers for measuring slope movements or settlement plates for detecting settlement in the ground. These devices aid in analyzing and predicting the behavior of soil and rock formations.</p></p>
