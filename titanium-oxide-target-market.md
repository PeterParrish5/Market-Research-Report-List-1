<p><p>The titanium oxide target market can be categorized into two types: plane target and rotating target. A plane target is a flat and stationary surface used for deposition processes, where the material is deposited evenly over the surface. This market mainly caters to industries requiring uniform coating for their products. On the other hand, a rotating target involves a cylindrical target that rotates during the deposition process. This market is ideal for industries that require high deposition rates and improved film uniformity, such as the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
