Glass wool is made by melting glass and forming fibers. Rockwool/stonewool is produced by melting rock and spinning it into fibers. EPS and XPS are plastic foams made from polystyrene. Polyurethane foams are derived from polymeric materials and offer high insulation properties. Polyisocyanurate is a rigid foam board with excellent thermal resistance. Phenolic foams consist of a phenolic resin and a blowing agent. These materials are used for thermal insulation in construction applications.</p></p>
