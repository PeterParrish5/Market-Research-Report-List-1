<p><p>Automatic lubricators or oilers are devices used to automatically supply lubrication to machinery or equipment. There are three main types of automatic lubricators: Oil Mist Type, Single Point Type, and Multipoint Type. </p><p>Oil Mist Type lubricators generate a fine mist of lubricating oil particles, which are then distributed through the machinery using a system of tubes or ducts. It is suitable for large or complex machinery that requires lubrication in multiple areas.</p><p>Single Point Type lubricators supply lubrication to a specific point or area of machinery. They are compact and often used in smaller machinery or equipment.</p><p>Multipoint Type lubricators are designed to supply lubrication to multiple points simultaneously. They typically have multiple outlets and are used in machinery or equipment that has several lubrication points.</p></p>
