<p><p>EMI/RFI Filters refer to electronic components designed to suppress electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI) in various electronic systems. There are three main market types for these filters: </p><p>1. Board Mount EMI/RFI Filters: These filters are embedded on the circuit board of electronic devices to remove unwanted electromagnetic noise generated within the device itself, ensuring smooth operation and high performance.</p><p>2. Power Line EMI/RFI Filters: These filters are installed on power lines to reduce the interference caused by electromagnetic radiation from power sources, ensuring clean power supply to sensitive electronic equipment and reducing the risk of malfunctions or data corruption.</p><p>3. Data Line EMI/RFI Filters: These filters are used on data communication lines, such as USB, HDMI, or Ethernet, to eliminate electromagnetic interference, enhancing signal quality, and preventing data loss or disruptions during transmission.</p></p>
