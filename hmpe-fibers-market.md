In the dry process, the fibers are produced using a melt spinning technique where the polymer is melted and extruded into filament form. The wet process, on the other hand, involves spinning the fibers in a solution and then solidifying them through a drying process. Both processes provide unique properties to the HMPE fibers, catering to different applications and market demands.</p></p>
